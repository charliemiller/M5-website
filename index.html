Founder & Managing Director

Charlie Miller

Charlie is the founder and managing director of M5 Advisors. With a background in digital strategy, product development, and user experience, he helps real estate leaders integrate technology to enhance experiences, optimize operations, and maximize asset value.

Before founding M5, Charlie was VP of Innovation & Digital Experience at Brookfield Asset Management, where he led digital master planning for large-scale developments. Prior to that he was Senior Director at Intersection — part of Sidewalk Labs and Alphabet — and a partner at Control Group, the design and technology consultancy behind the LinkNYC smart city network. For two decades his work has centered on reimagining the interaction between people, technology, and urban spaces.
